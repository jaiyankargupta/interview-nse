## üí° Pro Tips

### During Technical Interviews:
1. **Think aloud** - Show your problem-solving process
2. **Ask clarifying questions** - Don't make assumptions
3. **Start with brute force** - Then optimize
4. **Test your code** - Walk through examples
5. **Explain trade-offs** - Show you understand pros/cons

### During HR Interviews:
1. **Be authentic** - Genuine enthusiasm shows
2. **Tell stories** - Use STAR method
3. **Show passion** - For FinTech, for learning, for impact
4. **Ask insightful questions** - Shows you've researched
5. **Be honest about gaps** - But show eagerness to learn

### General Interview Tips:
1. **Listen carefully** - Understand what they're really asking
2. **Take your time** - Pause to think before answering
3. **Be specific** - Use concrete examples from your projects
4. **Stay positive** - Even when discussing failures
5. **Follow up** - Send thank-you email within 24 hours
